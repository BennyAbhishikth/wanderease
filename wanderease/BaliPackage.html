Itinerary

Morning:

  • Arrival: Land at Ngurah Rai International Airport, Bali.
  • Transfer to Hotel: Check-in at your hotel in the Uluwatu or Jimbaran area.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Enjoy a meal at a local restaurant or at your hotel.
  • Uluwatu Temple: Visit Uluwatu Temple, perched on a cliff with stunning ocean views. Explore the temple and watch the traditional Kecak dance performance in the evening.

Evening:

  • Dinner: Dine at a seaside restaurant in Jimbaran Bay, known for its fresh seafood.

Morning:

  • Breakfast: At your hotel.
  • Transfer to Nusa Penida: Take a fast boat from Sanur Beach to Nusa Penida Island.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Enjoy a meal at a local restaurant on Nusa Penida.
  • Nusa Penida Tour: Explore the island’s highlights, including:
    • Keling King Beach: Famous for its dramatic views and unique rock formations.
    • Angel’s Billabong: A natural infinity pool with clear water.
    • Broken Beach: A picturesque natural archway over the ocean.

Evening:

  • Return to Bali: Take the fast boat back to Bali.
  • Dinner: Relax and have dinner at your hotel or a nearby restaurant.

Morning:

  • Breakfast: At your hotel.
  • Waterfall Visit: Explore one of Bali's beautiful waterfalls, such as Tegenungan Waterfall.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant near the waterfalls.
  • ATV Adventure: Embark on an ATV adventure through Bali's scenic landscapes, including rice fields, forests, and rivers.

Evening:

  • Beach Club: Head to a popular beach club such as Potato Head Beach Club or Finns Beach Club for a relaxing evening by the ocean.
  • Dinner: Dine at the beach club or explore dining options in the area.

Morning:

  • Breakfast: At your hotel.
  • Transfer to Ubud: Travel to Ubud, Bali’s cultural heart.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Enjoy lunch at a café or restaurant in Ubud.
  • Rice Terraces: Explore the iconic Tegalalang Rice Terraces or Jatiluwih Rice Terraces, known for their stunning green landscapes and traditional rice farming techniques.
  • Ubud Market: Browse local crafts, souvenirs, and traditional Balinese items at Ubud Market.

Evening:

  • Dinner: Dine at a restaurant in Ubud, perhaps trying some traditional Balinese cuisine

Early Morning:

  • Mount Batur Sunrise Trek: Depart early for a sunrise trek up Mount Batur. Enjoy panoramic views as the sun rises over the volcanic landscape.

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Enjoy a breakfast with a view after the trek.

Late Morning:

  • Transfer to Tirta Ganga: Head to the Tirta Ganga Water Palace.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Have lunch at a local restaurant or café near Tirta Ganga.
  • Tirta Ganga: Explore the Tirta Ganga Water Palace, known for its serene pools and beautiful gardens.

Evening:

  • Return to Hotel: Head back to your hotel in Ubud or your base location.
  • Dinner: Relax and have dinner at your hotel or a nearby restaurant.

Morning:

  • Breakfast: At your hotel.
  • Garuda Wisnu Kencana: Visit the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park to see the massive Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue and enjoy the cultural performances and art exhibitions.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Have lunch at a restaurant in or near the cultural park.
  • Shopping or Relaxation: If time permits, do some last-minute shopping or relaxation at a nearby café.

Evening:

  • Transfer to Airport: Check out of your hotel and transfer to Ngurah Rai International Airport for your departure.
